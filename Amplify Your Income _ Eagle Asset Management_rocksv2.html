    The Suite

    Four Strategies.
    One Income Mandate.

    Each strategy in Eagle's Sources of Income Suite is independently managed with a dedicated research team — providing genuine diversification across income sources. Together they offer advisors a modular framework to build income-oriented allocations tailored to client risk profiles and time horizons.

    Fixed Income

    [Strategy Name 1]

    Core | Broad Market Income

    A diversified, actively managed fixed income strategy seeking consistent income generation across the full credit spectrum. Rigorous bottom-up credit research identifies compelling risk-adjusted opportunities with a disciplined focus on capital preservation alongside yield.

    Client Fit

    Conservative to moderate investors seeking predictable income with lower volatility. Ideal for clients in or approaching retirement who require income without undue principal risk.

    Equity Income

    [Strategy Name 2]

    Dividend Growth | Capital Appreciation

    A high-conviction equity strategy built around companies with durable dividend profiles and the financial strength to grow distributions over time. Seeks competitive total returns through a blend of current income and long-term capital appreciation.

    Client Fit

    Moderate risk clients seeking equity participation with a built-in income component. Ideal for clients who want growth potential alongside meaningful current yield, particularly those approaching retirement.

    Multi-Asset

    [Strategy Name 3]

    Blended | Flexible Allocation

    A flexible, multi-asset approach that dynamically allocates across equities, fixed income, and specialty income sources to pursue an elevated income target while managing downside risk. Tactical positioning responds to shifting market conditions without a fixed allocation constraint.

    Client Fit

    Moderate to moderately aggressive clients who require a higher income target with diversified risk management. A strong core holding for clients seeking a managed, all-in-one income solution.

    Short Duration

    [Strategy Name 4]

    Capital Preservation | Liquidity-Aware

    A capital preservation-oriented strategy focused on the short end of the yield curve — delivering income above cash alternatives while limiting interest rate sensitivity. Provides stability and liquidity without sacrificing meaningful yield in the current environment.

    Client Fit

    Conservative clients with near-term liquidity needs or as a cash alternative. Particularly timely for clients sitting on elevated idle assets who need a defensible, income-generating holding period solution.
