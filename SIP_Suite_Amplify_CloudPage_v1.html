Strategies

Three dynamic ways to deliver income

Designed to cover the income spectrum — from stability-first clients to those seeking higher yield and broader diversification.

SIP

Strategic Income Portfolio

Steady income and consistent growth — the suite’s income anchor.

  • Blends dividend-paying equities with investment-grade fixed income in one risk-aware portfolio.
  • Flexible allocation posture guided by macro indicators and fundamental insights.
  • Best fit: stability-focused income clients and core multi-asset lineups.
Download factsheet › Talk to your team ›
SIP Plus

Strategic Income Portfolio Plus

Increased income and steady growth — a structured “step-up” in yield potential.

  • Targets higher income via high-dividend equities and disciplined corporate credit exposure.
  • Fixed income sleeve focuses on BBB/BB credit with defined allocation ranges.
  • Best fit: yield-seeking clients willing to accept moderate volatility.
Download factsheet › Register for webinar ›
Global SIP

Global Strategic Income Portfolio

Global diversification and capital appreciation — broadens sources of income beyond U.S. markets.

  • Integrates U.S. dividend equities and investment-grade bonds with actively managed international dividend equities.
  • Designed to diversify income opportunity set across regions and sectors.
  • Best fit: clients seeking diversified income with an international component.
