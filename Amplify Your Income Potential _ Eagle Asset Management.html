    The SIP Suite

    One Disciplined Process.
    Three Dynamic Strategies.

    Each strategy within the suite is built on the same tactical, risk-conscious framework — differentiated by income objective, risk profile, and asset class exposure to meet clients where they are.

    Core Income

    SIP

    Seeks steady income and consistent growth

    Dividend-growth U.S. equities, investment-grade fixed income, and cash — actively balanced to pursue steady income and capital preservation. Allocation limits: 35–65% equities, 35–65% bonds, 0–30% cash.

    Client Fit

    Retirees seeking a steady paycheck and capital appreciation. The income anchor within multi-asset lineups for clients prioritizing stability and capital discipline.

    Conservative–Moderate Moderate Income

    Increased Income

    SIP Plus

    Seeks higher income potential

    Higher-yielding dividend U.S. equities, investment-grade and selective high-yield corporate credit (primarily BB), and cash — managed to seek higher income within a disciplined, quality-first framework.

    Client Fit

    Clients seeking increased cash flow and higher income potential who accept moderate volatility within a structured, quality-conscious framework.

    Moderate–High High Income

    Global Income

    Global SIP

    Seeks diversified, international income

    U.S. and international dividend equities, U.S. investment-grade fixed income, and cash — tactically managed to broaden and diversify income potential beyond U.S. markets.

    Client Fit

    Investors seeking global diversification with an income-oriented approach. Broadens income sources beyond U.S. markets by adding international dividend exposure.

    Our Approach

    A Fine-Tuned Approach to Income

    The SIP Suite is backed by a consistent bottom-up investment philosophy and active tactical management — built to pursue income without sacrificing discipline. Each strategy shares three core goals, executed with a team-specific approach to risk and opportunity.

    01

    Capital Preservation

    Disciplined risk management across equities, bonds, and cash as market conditions evolve — so income generation never comes at the cost of principal discipline.

    02

    Steady Income Production

    Consistently producing current and future income through dividend-producing stocks and fixed-income securities across multiple market cycles.

    03

    Broad Market Exposure

    Both equity and bond exposure with flexibility to adjust allocations to support income needs and maintain capital through changing market conditions.

    04

    Active Tactical Management

    Tenured investment teams with the autonomy to pursue decisions guided by their individual philosophies — and the discipline to act within a unified framework.
