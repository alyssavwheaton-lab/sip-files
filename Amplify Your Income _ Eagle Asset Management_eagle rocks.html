Equity Income

Equity Income Strategy

Dividend Growth | Capital Appreciation

A high-conviction equity strategy built around companies with durable dividend profiles and the financial strength to grow those distributions over time. Seeks to deliver competitive total returns through a blend of current income and long-term capital appreciation.

Client Fit

Moderate risk clients seeking equity participation with a built-in income component. Ideal for clients who want growth potential alongside meaningful current yield — particularly those in the accumulation phase approaching retirement.

Multi-Asset

[Strategy Name 3]
Multi-Asset Income Strategy

Blended | Flexible Allocation

A flexible, multi-asset approach that dynamically allocates across equities, fixed income, and specialty income sources to pursue an elevated income target while managing downside risk. Tactical positioning allows the team to respond to shifting market conditions without being constrained to a fixed allocation model.

Client Fit

Moderate to moderately aggressive clients who require a higher income target but benefit from diversified risk management. A strong core holding for clients seeking a managed, all-in-one income solution without the complexity of a multi-manager structure.

Short Duration

[Strategy Name 4]
Short Duration Strategy

Capital Preservation | Liquidity-Aware

A capital preservation-oriented fixed income strategy focused on the short end of the yield curve. Designed to deliver income above cash alternatives while limiting interest rate sensitivity — providing stability and liquidity without sacrificing meaningful yield in the current environment.

Client Fit

Conservative clients, near-term liquidity needs, or as a cash alternative for clients sitting on elevated idle assets. Particularly timely for clients waiting to redeploy capital who require a defensible, income-generating holding period solution.
