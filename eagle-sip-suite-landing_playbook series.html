Core Income

SIP

Seeks steady income and consistent growth

Dividend-growth U.S. equities, investment-grade fixed income, and cash — actively balanced to pursue steady income and capital preservation. Allocation limits: 35–65% equities, 35–65% bonds, 0–30% cash.

Retirees seeking a steady paycheck and capital appreciation. The income anchor within multi-asset lineups for clients prioritizing stability and capital discipline.

Our Approach

A Fine-Tuned Approach to Income

The SIP Suite is backed by a consistent bottom-up investment philosophy and active tactical management — built to pursue income without sacrificing discipline. Each strategy shares three core goals, executed with a team-specific approach to risk and opportunity.

01

Capital Preservation

Disciplined risk management across equities, bonds, and cash as market conditions evolve — income generation that never comes at the cost of principal discipline.

02

Steady Income Production

Consistently producing current and future income through dividend-producing stocks and fixed-income securities across multiple market cycles.

03

Broad Market Exposure

Both equity and bond exposure with flexibility to adjust allocations to support income needs and maintain capital through changing market conditions.

04

Active Tactical Management

Tenured investment teams with the autonomy to pursue decisions guided by their individual philosophies — and the discipline to act within a unified framework.
