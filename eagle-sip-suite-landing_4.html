    The SIP Suite

    One Disciplined Process.
    Three Dynamic Strategies.

    Help amplify your clients' income through broader diversification and higher-yield opportunities — all with the same tactical, risk-conscious core SIP approach. Each strategy is designed to serve a distinct client profile within a unified, quality-first income framework.

    Core Income

    SIP

    Seeks steady income and consistent growth

    Dividend-growth U.S. equities, investment-grade fixed income, and cash — actively balanced to pursue steady income and capital preservation within a structured allocation framework (35–65% equities / 35–65% bonds / 0–30% cash).

    Client Fit

    Retirees seeking a steady paycheck and capital appreciation. The income anchor within multi-asset lineups for clients prioritizing stability and capital discipline.

    Increased Income

    SIP Plus

    Seeks higher income potential

    Higher-yielding dividend U.S. equities, investment-grade and selective high-yield corporate credit (primarily BB), and cash — managed to seek higher income within a disciplined, quality-first framework.

    Client Fit

    Clients seeking increased cash flow and higher income potential who accept moderate volatility for potentially higher income within a structured, quality-conscious framework.

    Global Income

    Global SIP

    Seeks diversified, international income

    U.S. and international dividend equities, U.S. investment-grade fixed income, and cash — tactically managed to broaden and diversify income potential beyond U.S. markets with international dividend exposure.

    Client Fit

    Investors seeking global diversification with an income-oriented approach. Broadens income sources beyond U.S. markets by adding international dividend exposure and diversifying the income opportunity set.

    A Fine-Tuned Approach to Income

    Three Core Goals. One Unified Framework.

    Backed by consistent bottom-up investment philosophy and active tactical management, the SIP Suite is built to pursue income without sacrificing discipline.

    01

    Capital Preservation

    Seeks to preserve capital through disciplined risk management across equities, bonds, and cash as market conditions evolve — so income generation never comes at the cost of principal discipline.

    02

    Steady Income Production

    Aims to consistently produce current and future income through dividend-producing stocks and fixed-income securities — providing the reliability clients need through multiple market cycles.

    03

    Broad Market Exposure

    Provides both equity and bond exposure with the flexibility to adjust allocations to support income needs and maintain capital for clients through changing market conditions.

    Diversified income sources across equities, fixed income, and cash
